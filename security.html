Your Data is in Safe Hands

We know how critical your data is to you and that you rely on us to keep it secure. Our service is designed to satisfy the security needs of SMEs to enterprises, so security is at the forefront of our thoughts as it is yours.

Data Security

Interpretive employs the latest security technologies. Our data is hosted on Google’s Cloud Platform located in Europe, which has completed ISO 27001 audits to assure optimal security methods and procedures.

Communications

All communications with interpretive are transmitted over SSL (HTTPS/SFTP) for both access to the public website as well as the API. We can also provide two-factor authentication and PGP keys to verify each party’s identities.

Employee Access

Data privacy and integrity is of the utmost concern at interpretive. As a result of our security model, only our data science team would ever have direct access to client data.

Physical Security

We use only premier data center facilities. Each site is staffed 24/7/365 with onsite security and to protect against unauthorized entry.
